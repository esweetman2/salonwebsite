Linda Sweetman Hair Care

About

Linda is a veteran hairdresser having spent much of her career focused on hair color. She has recieved extensive training from several hair color manufacturers, bringing chemical knowledge and creative depth to her work.

Approach

when designing a haircut Linda takes a somewhat organic approach, paying attention to the natural wave and growth directions of the hair. This makes for easier styling especially for those who like to wash and go.

Services

while providing a full range of hair color services, Linda's work is focused primarily on techniques for grey hair. Grey blending, grey coverage, highlights and lowlights, enhancing the beauty of natural grey hair. Also, linda enjoys helping women who are looking to transition from color treated hair to their natural grey.

Haircut and styling services are available, also permanent waving; soft waves to strong curls.
